Welcome to WebRTC

Start a private room between you and another peer, where you can communicate in real time through your browser or phone.


Getting started

  • Type a channel name and click Create
  • Get your colleague to open this page in another tab
  • They must type the same channel name in the other tab page and click Join
  • Start chatting!
×
How does this app work?

This app teaches you about WebRTC to establish direct, real-time communication through your browser with another peer. The other peer will also be connected by having an instance of this app open on their device.
